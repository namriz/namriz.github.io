About Us

Overview

This website features the religious sisters and diocesan priests in the Diocese of Tagbilaran. It features their journey in living religious life vocation. It also gives information about pursuing religious life. It provide quotes about religious life vocation in order to inspire and encourage teens and young adults to enter and pursue religious life vocation.

Mission/Goals:

-To help the religious in their mission which is to encourage teens and young adults to enter and pursue religious life vocation.

-To feature the beauty, goodness and importance of pursuing religious life vocation.
